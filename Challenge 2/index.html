Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Grease and flour a 9x9-inch pan or line with parchment paper.
  3. Beat sugar and butter together in a bowl using an electric mixer until smooth and creamy.
  4. Beat eggs, 1 at a time, into creamed butter mixture until just blended.
  5. Stir flour, milk, and baking powder into butter mixture until batter is smooth; pour into prepared pan.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 30 to 40 minutes.
